My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Greatest Showman

The Greatest Showman is a charming, spectacular musical and dance movie with valuable themes about the following: healing conflict, finding reconciliation, showing kindness, discovering and giving real love, unconditional love, and more.

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

The anime has a beautiful animation design, its funny and the characters are unique and interesting. And oh my god the music/soundtrack is just phennomenal

Guardian: The Lonely and Great God

"Guardian: The Lonely and Great God," also known as "Goblin," is a mesmerizing South Korean drama that has taken the world by storm. This enchanting masterpiece effortlessly weaves together fantasy, romance, and folklore in a way that will leave you spellbound.
